A Venue for All Your Needs

From weddings to the plays, baby showers to birthdays, business conferences to holiday parties, The Right Place can do it all!

What Makes Us the Best

Innovative Technology

Enhanced audio and visual, HD screens and more. We’ve got the connections to set you up. Need WiFi? We have you covered. Free wifi throughout the buildings!

Open Vendor Policy

Whatever your style, we accept and accommodate any vendor. Our neutral palette decor allows you to create most anything. Your preference is ours.

Flexible Floor Plans

We offer spacious floor plans, along with options for outdoor events. We make our space meet the needs of your event, large or small, no worries and no hassle.
